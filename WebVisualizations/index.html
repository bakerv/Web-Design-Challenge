Latitude vs. Weather - Is there a correlation?

Here we explore the relationship between weather and distance from the equator.

Data was collected from the OpenWeatherMap API for more than 500 randomly selected cities around the globe. To elimate the effects of day and night cycles, forecast data for the same day was used for all locations.

Tools Used:

Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, NumPy, SciPy, Requests, CitiPy, HTML5, and Bootstrap
